🤖 Android Device Instructions

1

Go to Settings > About Phone

2

Tap on "Status" or "Device Information"

3

Find and note your IMEI or Device ID

⚠️ Note: Android devices don't have UDID like iOS. Use IMEI or Android ID instead.
