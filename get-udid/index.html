Frequently Asked Questions

What is a UDID?
UDID (Unique Device Identifier) is a 40-character code unique to each iOS device, used by developers for beta testing and app provisioning.
Is this service secure?
Yes! The configuration profile redirects directly to our page with your UDID. We don't store any information on our servers.
Why do I need my UDID?
Developers need your UDID to add your device to their Apple Developer account for beta testing and app signing.
How to remove the profile?
Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management, select the profile and tap "Remove Profile".
